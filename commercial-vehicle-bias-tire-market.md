<p><p>Commercial Vehicle Bias Tire Market is divided into two main types: Ordinary Bias Tires and Bias Belted Tires. Ordinary Bias Tires are traditional, heavy-duty tires made with multiple layers of rubber-coated fabric cords laid at angles perpendicular to each other. These tires offer durability and stability at a lower cost. On the other hand, Bias Belted Tires have an additional layer of belts beneath the tread. This belt helps improve the tire's performance, offering better traction, handling, and longer tread life.</p></p>
