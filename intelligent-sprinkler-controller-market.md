<p><p>Intelligent sprinkler controllers can be categorized into two main types: weather-based controllers and sensor-based controllers. Weather-based controllers utilize real-time weather data to adjust watering schedules according to current weather conditions. On the other hand, sensor-based controllers rely on sensors to detect moisture levels in the soil and adjust watering accordingly. Both types of controllers aim to optimize water usage, prevent over watering, and ensure proper irrigation, leading to more efficient and sustainable landscaping practices.</p></p>
