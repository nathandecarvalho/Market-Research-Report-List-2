<p><p>Laser protection windows are essential for safeguarding individuals from harmful laser radiation. They come in three main types: Polycarbonate Plate, Acrylic Plate, and Glass Plate. Polycarbonate plates are lightweight and impact-resistant, making them suitable for high-risk environments. Acrylic plates offer excellent optical clarity and are scratch-resistant, making them ideal for precision applications. Each type of window serves a specific purpose and provides varying levels of protection depending on the environment and exposure to laser radiation.</p></p>
