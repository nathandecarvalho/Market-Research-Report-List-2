<p><strong>The PEX-EVOH Pipe Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>PEX-A Pipes</li><li>PEX-B Pipes</li><li>PEX-C Pipes</li><li>PEX-AL-PEX Pipes</li></ul></p>
<p><p>PEX-EVOH pipe market comprises four main types: PEX-A, PEX-B, PEX-C, and PEX-AL-PEX pipes. PEX-A pipes are cross-linked using the Engel method, making them highly flexible and resilient. PEX-B pipes are cross-linked using the Silane method, offering good flexibility and affordability. PEX-C pipes are cross-linked using electron beam processing, providing excellent temperature and pressure resistance. PEX-AL-PEX pipes have an aluminum layer, offering enhanced durability and oxygen barrier properties. Each type caters to different needs and preferences in the market.</p></p>
