There are two main types of sensors commonly used in the market - Hall Type and Magnetic Electric Type. Hall Type sensors use Hall effect technology to detect changes in magnetic fields, while Magnetic Electric Type sensors work based on changes in magnetic fields generated by the rotation of the wheel.
