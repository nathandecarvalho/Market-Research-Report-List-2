<p><p>Emissions control catalysts are devices used to reduce harmful emissions from vehicles and industrial processes. The three main types of emissions control catalysts are honeycomb catalysts, plate catalysts, and corrugated catalysts. Honeycomb catalysts are structured like their namesake, with a honeycomb-like structure that allows for high surface area and efficient conversion of pollutants. Plate catalysts are flat plates coated with catalytic materials, while corrugated catalysts have a ridged surface that promotes turbulence and enhances reaction rates. Each type has its own advantages and applications in emissions control.</p></p>
