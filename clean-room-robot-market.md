<p><p>Clean Room Robot market consists of various types of robots including Articulated Robots, SCARA Robots, Parallel Robots, Cartesian Robots, and Collaborative Robots. Articulated Robots have multiple joints, allowing for complex movements and flexibility. SCARA Robots are best suited for fast and precise operations. Parallel Robots offer high speed and accuracy for pick-and-place applications. Cartesian Robots have a simple, linear movement ideal for repetitive tasks. Collaborative Robots are designed to work safely alongside humans, promoting efficiency and productivity in clean room environments.</p></p>
