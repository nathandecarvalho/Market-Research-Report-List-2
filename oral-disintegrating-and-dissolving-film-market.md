<p><p>Oral disintegrating films (ODFs) and dissolving films are innovative drug delivery systems that are designed to dissolve or disintegrate rapidly in the oral cavity. These films offer advantages such as easy administration, rapid onset of action, and improved patient compliance. Orodispersible films are placed on the tongue where they disintegrate quickly, while oromucosal films are designed to dissolve in the buccal cavity. Both types of films are gaining popularity in the pharmaceutical industry due to their convenience and effectiveness.</p></p>
