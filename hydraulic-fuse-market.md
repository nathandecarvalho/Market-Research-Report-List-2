<p><strong>The Hydraulic Fuse Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Pressure Relief Type</li><li>Reflux Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Hydraulic fuse market includes two main types: pressure relief type and reflux type. Pressure relief type hydraulic fuses are designed to release excess pressure to prevent damage to the system, while reflux type hydraulic fuses stop the flow of hydraulic fluid in the event of a leak. Both types play a crucial role in maintaining the safety and efficiency of hydraulic systems by protecting against overpressure situations and fluid loss. The choice of hydraulic fuse type depends on specific system requirements and operating conditions.</p></p>
