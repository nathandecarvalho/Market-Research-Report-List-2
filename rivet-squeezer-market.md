<p><p>A rivet squeezer is a type of tool used in metalworking to fasten two pieces of material together by deforming a rivet. It operates by squeezing the two pieces of material between a die and a bucking bar, causing the rivet to deform and secure the joint. Rivet squeezers are commonly used in industries such as aerospace, automotive, and construction.</p><p>The Rivet Squeezer Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 12.5% during the forecast period.
