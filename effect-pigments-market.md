<p><p>Effect pigments are classified into two main types - Natural Mineral Pigments and Artificial Mineral Pigments. Natural Mineral Pigments are derived from naturally occurring minerals like mica, iron oxide, and titanium dioxide. These pigments provide vibrant colors and unique effects to products. On the other hand, Artificial Mineral Pigments are produced synthetically in laboratories using chemical processes. They offer a wider range of colors and effects, making them popular in various industries such as cosmetics, automotive, and coatings.</p></p>
