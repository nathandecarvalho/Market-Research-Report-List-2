<p><strong>The Steel Plate Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Hot Rolled Steel Plate</li><li>Cold Rolled Steel Plate</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Steel plate market can be categorized into two main types: Hot Rolled Steel Plate and Cold Rolled Steel Plate. Hot rolled steel plates are produced by heating steel slabs and rolling them to achieve the desired thickness and shape. They are generally used in applications where precision is not as critical. On the other hand, Cold rolled steel plates are manufactured by passing hot rolled steel through a cold reduction process, resulting in a smoother surface finish and higher dimensional accuracy. They are commonly used in applications that require precise dimensions and a smooth surface finish.</p></p>
