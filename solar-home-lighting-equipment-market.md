<p><strong>The Solar Home Lighting Equipment Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Off-grid Solar</li><li>Grid-tied Solar</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Off-grid Solar Home Lighting Equipment refers to standalone solar systems that are not connected to the main electricity grid. These systems are mostly used in remote areas where access to the grid is limited. On the other hand, Grid-tied Solar Home Lighting Equipment is connected to the main electricity grid and can feed excess electricity back into the grid. These systems are more common in urban areas where grid connectivity is easily available. Both types of solar home lighting equipment provide sustainable and eco-friendly lighting solutions for households.</p></p>
