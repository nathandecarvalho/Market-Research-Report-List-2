<p><p>Fiber Optical Transmitters are available in different types such as SFP, SFP+, QSFP/QSFP+, XFP, and CFP/CXP. SFP (Small Form-Factor Pluggable) transceivers are commonly used for data communication applications, while SFP+ offers higher data rates. QSFP/QSFP+ transceivers provide even higher speeds and are suitable for high-performance data centers. XFP transceivers are used for 10Gbps links, while CFP/CXP transceivers are designed for ultra-high-speed applications. Each type has specific features and applications to cater to different market needs.</p></p>
