<p><p>Micro Denier Fiber market can be segmented into two main types based on the coating used: Silicone Micro Denier Fiber and Non-silicone Micro Denier Fiber. Silicone Micro Denier Fiber is coated with silicone which gives it properties such as water repellency and softness, making it suitable for use in products like outdoor clothing and bedding. On the other hand, Non-silicone Micro Denier Fiber does not have a silicone coating and is commonly used in applications where water repellency is not a priority, such as in upholstery and carpets.</p></p>
