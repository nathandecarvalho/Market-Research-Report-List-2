<p><p>Machine vision light sources are crucial in providing illumination for imaging systems to capture high-quality images. The three main types of light sources used in machine vision applications are LED, halogen, and fluorescent lights. LED lights are energy-efficient, long-lasting, and offer precise control over lighting intensity. Halogen lights are bright and provide a consistent color temperature, but have a shorter lifespan. Fluorescent lights are cost-effective, but may not offer the same level of brightness or color accuracy as LED or halogen lights.</p></p>
