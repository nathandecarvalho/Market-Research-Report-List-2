<p><p>Functional Electrical Stimulation Equipment Market includes two types - Wired Equipment and Wireless Equipment. On the other hand, Wireless Equipment operates without the need for physical wires, offering more freedom of movement for the user. Both types of equipment are used in rehabilitation and therapy to stimulate muscles and improve motor function in individuals with neurological disorders or injuries.</p></p>
