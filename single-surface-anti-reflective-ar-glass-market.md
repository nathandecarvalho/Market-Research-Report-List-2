<p><p>Single surface anti-reflective (AR) glass is available in three main market types: transparent, translucent, and other. Transparent AR glass allows maximum light transmission and clarity, making it ideal for applications where visibility and aesthetics are important. Translucent AR glass provides a balance between light transmission and diffusing properties, commonly used in privacy screens or decorative elements. The "other" market includes specialty AR glass with unique features like color tinting or impact resistance, catering to specialized applications such as automotive or architectural design.</p></p>
