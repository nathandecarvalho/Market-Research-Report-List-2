<p><p>The Car VRLA battery market includes two main types - AGM (Absorbent Glass Mat) batteries and Gel batteries. AGM batteries have glass fiber mats that absorb the electrolyte, providing high power output and fast recharge capability. Gel batteries have silica gel electrolytes that offer better resistance to vibration and deep discharges. Both types of batteries are maintenance-free, leak-proof, and excel in applications requiring reliable power supply, such as in vehicles and UPS systems. The choice between AGM and Gel batteries depends on specific requirements and operating conditions.</p></p>
