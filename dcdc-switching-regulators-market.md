<p><p>DC/DC switching regulators are devices that convert one DC voltage to another. The four main types of DC/DC switching regulators are Buck, Boost, Buck/Boost, and Invert. Buck regulators step down the input voltage, Boost regulators step up the input voltage, Buck/Boost regulators can step up or step down the input voltage, and Invert regulators produce a negative output voltage. Each type of regulator is used in various applications depending on the desired output voltage and current requirements.</p></p>
