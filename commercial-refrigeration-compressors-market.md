<p><p>Commercial refrigeration compressors are categorized into three main types: reciprocating compressors, rotary compressors, and other types. Reciprocating compressors use a piston-cylinder arrangement to create refrigerant compression. Rotary compressors operate by using rotating motion to compress the refrigerant. Other types of compressors include scroll compressors, centrifugal compressors, and screw compressors. Each type of compressor has its unique advantages and disadvantages, making them suitable for different applications in the commercial refrigeration industry.</p></p>
