<p><p>Flat glass market consists of three main types: ordinary flat glass, float glass, and rolled glass. Ordinary flat glass is a basic type of glass that is commonly used in windows and various household items. Float glass is a high-quality, smooth, and distortion-free type of glass made by floating molten glass on a bed of molten tin. Rolled glass is a type of textured glass that is produced by rolling the molten glass on a metal table to create various patterns and textures.</p></p>
