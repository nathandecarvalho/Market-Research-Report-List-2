<p><p>Building waterproof material market types include:</p><p>1. Cementitious Waterproofing: A type of waterproofing that utilizes cement-based materials to create a protective barrier against water penetration.</p><p>2. Liquid Waterproofing Membrane: A liquid-applied membrane that forms a seamless waterproof barrier when dried.</p><p>3. Bituminous Membrane: A waterproofing membrane made from bitumen and reinforced with polyester or fiberglass.</p><p>4. Bituminous Coating: A coating made from bitumen that provides waterproofing by forming a durable and flexible protective layer.</p><p>5. Polyurethane Liquid Membrane: A liquid membrane made from polyurethane that provides excellent waterproofing and chemical resistance.</p></p>
