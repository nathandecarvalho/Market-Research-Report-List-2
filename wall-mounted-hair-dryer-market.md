<p><p>The wall mounted hair dryer market can be divided into two main types: centrifugal hair dryers and axial hair dryers. Centrifugal hair dryers use a rotating fan to create airflow and distribute heat evenly, resulting in quicker drying times. On the other hand, axial hair dryers have a straight airflow path that provides a more concentrated drying experience. Both types cater to different consumer preferences and hair types, offering various features and benefits to suit individual needs.</p></p>
