<p><p>Cementitious concrete repair mortars are widely used in construction for repairing damaged concrete structures. There are two main types of cementitious concrete repair mortars - Portland cement mortar and polymer modified cement mortar. Portland cement mortar is made from a mixture of Portland cement, sand, and water, while polymer modified cement mortar includes additional polymer additives to improve bonding, flexibility, and durability. Both types of mortars are essential for addressing concrete cracks, spalls, and other damage to maintain structural integrity and prolong the lifespan of concrete structures.</p></p>
