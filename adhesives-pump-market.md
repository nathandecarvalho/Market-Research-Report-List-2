<p><p>Adhesives pump market is segmented into different types such as diaphragm pump, lobe pump, and others. Diaphragm pumps use a flexible diaphragm to create suction and discharge, making them suitable for high-viscosity adhesives. Lobe pumps use two rotating lobes to push adhesives through the pump, offering gentle handling of shear-sensitive materials. Other types of pumps in the market may include gear pumps, peristaltic pumps, and piston pumps, each with unique features suited for specific adhesive applications.</p></p>
