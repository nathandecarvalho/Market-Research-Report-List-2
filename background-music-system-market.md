<p><p>Background music systems can be categorized into two main types: Central and Split systems. Central systems involve a single source of music that is distributed throughout a space using speakers placed strategically in different areas. On the other hand, Split systems allow for different sources of music to play simultaneously in distinct zones through separate speaker systems. Both types cater to different needs and preferences, offering flexibility in controlling and customizing the music playback in various settings.</p></p>
