<p><p>Mountain dulcimers can be classified into two main types based on the wood used in their construction: all solid wood and laminated wood. All solid wood dulcimers are made from a single piece of high-quality wood, offering a rich and resonant tone. These dulcimers are often preferred by professional musicians for their superior sound quality. On the other hand, laminated wood dulcimers are made from multiple layers of wood pressed together, offering durability and affordability. They are a popular choice for beginners or casual players looking for a budget-friendly option.</p></p>
