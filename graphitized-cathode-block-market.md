<p><strong>The Graphitized Cathode Block Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Semi-Graphitic</li><li>Graphitic</li><li>Graphitized</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Semi-graphitic cathode blocks are partially graphitized and contain a mix of graphite and other carbon materials. Graphitic cathode blocks have a higher graphite content compared to semi-graphitic blocks, resulting in improved thermal conductivity and electrical resistivity. Fully graphitized cathode blocks have the highest graphite content, providing superior performance in terms of electrical and thermal conductivity. These different types cater to varying needs in the aluminum industry, with graphitized blocks being the most sought after for high-performance applications.</p></p>
