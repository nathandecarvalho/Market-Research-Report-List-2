<p><p>Double-sided printed circuit boards (PCBs) are made using different types of materials such as glass fibre, paper, metal, ceramics, and others. Glass fibre PCBs are the most common and widely used due to their durability and cost-effectiveness. Paper PCBs are inexpensive but less durable. Metal PCBs offer good heat dissipation and are suitable for high-power applications. Ceramic PCBs are highly reliable and provide excellent thermal properties. Other materials such as composites and plastics are also used for specific applications in the double-sided PCB market.</p></p>
