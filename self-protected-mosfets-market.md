<p><ul><li>Low Side Devices</li><li>High Side Devices</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Self-protected MOSFETs are available in two main types: Low Side Devices and High Side Devices. Low Side Devices are designed to be connected between the load and ground, while High Side Devices are connected between the load and the power supply. Low Side Devices are common in applications where the load is grounded, while High Side Devices are used when the load is connected to a positive voltage source. Both types provide protection features like overcurrent, overtemperature, and overvoltage protection.</p></p>
