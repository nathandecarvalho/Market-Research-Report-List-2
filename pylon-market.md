<p><p>Pylon market types refer to different designs and functions of electricity towers. Tension towers are tall structures that support overhead power lines with tension cables. Straight towers are standard towers that support power lines in a straight line. Corner towers are used to change the direction of power lines. Transposition towers allow power lines to switch positions. Terminal towers mark the end points of power lines. Spanning towers are used to cover long distances and span across landscapes. Each type serves a specific purpose in the transmission of electricity.</p></p>
