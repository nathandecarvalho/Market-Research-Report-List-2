<p><p>Battery powered chainsaws can be categorized into two main types - corded and cordless chainsaws. Corded chainsaws are powered by electricity and need to be connected to a power source, limiting their portability. On the other hand, cordless chainsaws run on rechargeable batteries, offering more freedom of movement without the need for a power outlet. Cordless chainsaws are often preferred for their convenience and versatility, while corded chainsaws are typically used for heavier-duty tasks that require continuous power supply.</p></p>
