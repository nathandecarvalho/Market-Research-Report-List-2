<p><p>Hydroxypropyl Cellulose (HPC) is a modified cellulose polymer used in various industries. There are two main types of HPC in the market: L-HPC and H-HPC. L-HPC has lower viscosity and is commonly used in food, cosmetics, and pharmaceuticals.
