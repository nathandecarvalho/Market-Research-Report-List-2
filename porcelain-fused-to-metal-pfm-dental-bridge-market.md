<p><p>Porcelain Fused to Metal (PFM) Dental Bridge market includes various types based on their design and structure. Traditional PFM bridges are supported by adjacent natural teeth, while Cantilever bridges are supported by only one natural tooth. Maryland bridges involve resin or metal wings attached to the back of adjacent teeth for support. Implant-supported bridges are secured to dental implants, offering a more stable and long-lasting solution. Each type caters to specific patient needs and preferences in the dental bridge market.</p></p>
