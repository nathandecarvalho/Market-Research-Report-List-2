<p><p>Organic insulation materials market consists of various types such as Expanded Polystyrene Board (EPS), Extruded Polystyrene Board (XPS), Spray Polyurethane (SPU), Polystyrene Particles, and Other materials. EPS and XPS boards are rigid foam insulation commonly used in buildings. SPU is a spray-applied foam insulation known for its high R-value. Polystyrene particles are loose-fill insulation used in walls and attics. Other materials in the market include cotton, wool, and cellulose insulation, which are natural and eco-friendly options for insulation purposes.</p></p>
