<p><ul><li>Diaphragm</li><li>Piston</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Linear valve actuators are classified into two main types - diaphragm and piston actuators. Diaphragm actuators use a flexible diaphragm to control the movement of the valve, while piston actuators utilize a piston mechanism to complete the same function. Diaphragm actuators are known for their simplicity and cost-effectiveness, making them suitable for basic applications. On the other hand, piston actuators offer higher precision and control, making them ideal for more demanding and sensitive operations in industries such as oil and gas, pharmaceuticals, and water treatment.</p></p>
