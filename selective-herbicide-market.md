<p><strong>The Selective Herbicide Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Chlorophenoxy Acid Herbicides</li><li>Triazine Herbicides</li><li>Organic Phosphorus Herbicides</li><li>Other</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Selective herbicides are classified into different types based on their chemical composition and mode of action. Chlorophenoxy acid herbicides, such as 2,4-D and MCPA, target broadleaf weeds while leaving grasses unharmed. Triazine herbicides, like atrazine and simazine, are effective at controlling both broadleaf and grassy weeds. Organic phosphorus herbicides, including glyphosate and glufosinate, inhibit plant enzyme activity. Other types of selective herbicides may include sulfonylureas, imidazolinones, and aryloxyphenoxypropionates, each with their specific target weeds and mode of action.</p></p>
