<p><p>The paper tester market can be categorized into two main types: physical testers and optical testers. Physical testers are used to measure the physical properties of paper such as thickness, strength, and absorbency. These testers are essential for ensuring the quality and reliability of paper products. On the other hand, optical testers are used to evaluate the optical properties of paper such as brightness, color, and opacity. Both types of testers play a crucial role in the paper industry by providing accurate and reliable data for quality control and product development.</p></p>
