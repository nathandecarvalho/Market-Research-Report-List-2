<p><p>Wireless barcode scanners are available in two types: Wifi-based and Cellular-based models. Wifi-based scanners utilize a local wireless network to transmit data instantly to a connected device, allowing for real-time tracking and inventory management. On the other hand, Cellular-based scanners use cellular data networks to transmit information, making them suitable for mobile applications where Wifi may not be available. Both types of scanners offer flexibility and convenience in various industries such as retail, logistics, and healthcare.</p></p>
