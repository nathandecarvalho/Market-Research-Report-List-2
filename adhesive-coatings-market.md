<p><strong>The Adhesive Coatings Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Non-Reactive Based Adhesive</li><li>Solvent Based Adhesive</li><li>Water-based Dispersion Adhesive</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Adhesive coatings market can be divided into different types based on the composition of the adhesives used. Non-reactive based adhesives are pre-formed and do not require any chemical reactions to adhere to surfaces. Solvent-based adhesives contain solvents which evaporate upon application to create a bond. Water-based dispersion adhesives use water as a carrier for the adhesive components. Other types of adhesives may include hot melt adhesives and pressure-sensitive adhesives. Each type of adhesive coating offers unique benefits and applications in various industries.</p></p>
