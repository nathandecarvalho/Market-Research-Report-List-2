Temperature sensor bearings measure the temperature of the bearing to prevent overheating. Vibration sensor bearings detect any abnormal vibrations in the machinery. Displacement sensor bearings measure the position of the bearing for accurate alignment. There are also other sensor bearing units available in the market that cater to specific requirements of different industries.</p></p>
