<p><p>Polyethylene pellets market is segmented into different types based on the molecular structure and properties. High-density polyethylene (HDPE) pellets are known for their strength and stiffness, making them ideal for packaging and pipes. Linear low-density polyethylene (LLDPE) pellets are flexible and puncture-resistant, commonly used in film and packaging. Low-density polyethylene (LDPE) pellets offer good clarity and flexibility, commonly used in packaging and tubing. The "Others" category includes specialty polyethylene pellets with specific properties for industrial applications.</p></p>
