<p><strong>The Nickel Tube Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Pure Nickel Tube</li><li>Alloy Nickel Tube</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Nickel tubes are available in two main market types: Pure Nickel Tubes and Alloy Nickel Tubes. Pure Nickel Tubes are made entirely of nickel and are highly resistant to corrosion and oxidation. They are commonly used in applications where purity is crucial, such as in chemical processing or electric component manufacturing. On the other hand, Alloy Nickel Tubes are a combination of nickel and other metals, offering enhanced strength, heat resistance, and other properties tailored to specific industrial needs, such as in aerospace or automotive applications.</p></p>
