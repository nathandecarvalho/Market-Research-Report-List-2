<p><p>The elevator door market consists of various types such as automatic doors, telescopic doors, swing doors, collapsible doors, and imperforated doors. Automatic doors open and close automatically, providing convenient access. Telescopic doors consist of multiple panels that slide open to maximize space. Swing doors open and close like traditional doors, while collapsible doors fold to the sides when not in use. Imperforated doors are solid doors without any openings. Each type of elevator door caters to different needs and preferences in the market.</p></p>
