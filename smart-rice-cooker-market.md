<p><strong>The Smart Rice Cooker Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Traditional Rice Cooker</li><li>IH (Induction Heating) Rice Cookers</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Traditional rice cookers are the basic and most common type of rice cookers available in the market. They operate by heating a coil located at the bottom of the cooker to cook the rice. On the other hand, IH (Induction Heating) rice cookers use electromagnetic technology to heat the entire pot, resulting in more evenly cooked rice. IH rice cookers are known for their precise temperature control and ability to cook rice at a faster speed compared to traditional rice cookers.</p></p>
