<p><p>Standard recovery diode rectifiers are classified based on their reverse recovery current rating. The market types include 2.5µA, 5µA, 10µA, and Others. The reverse recovery current rating indicates the amount of current that flows in the reverse direction during the recovery process of the diode. Diodes with lower reverse recovery current ratings are more efficient and have faster switching speeds.
