<p><p>Wave energy devices market includes different types such as point absorbers, oscillating water columns, oscillating body converters, overtopping wave power devices, and others. Point absorbers use buoyant structures to capture wave energy, oscillating water columns use air movement to generate electricity, oscillating body converters convert wave motion into mechanical energy, and overtopping wave power devices capture wave energy as it flows over a barrier. Other devices may include wave snakes, terminator devices, or hybrid systems combining different technologies to harness wave energy.</p></p>
