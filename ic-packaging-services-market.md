<p><strong>The IC Packaging Services Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Traditional Packaging</li><li>Advanced Packaging</li></ul></p>
<p><p>IC packaging services encompass traditional packaging and advanced packaging markets. Traditional packaging involves standard integrated circuit (IC) packaging such as dual in-line packages (DIP) and quad flat packages (QFP). It is ideal for simpler ICs and offers cost-effective solutions. On the other hand, the advanced packaging market involves innovative technologies such as system-in-package (SIP) and wafer-level packaging (WLP). These advanced packaging techniques cater to complex ICs requiring higher performance and miniaturization. Both markets play a crucial role in meeting the diverse packaging needs of the semiconductor industry.</p></p>
