<p><p>High purity quartz glass market is categorized into two main types: transparent quartz glass and opaque quartz glass. Transparent quartz glass is known for its high purity and excellent optical properties, making it ideal for applications such as lenses, prisms, and windows. On the other hand, opaque quartz glass is valued for its superior thermal insulation and resistance to high temperatures, making it suitable for use in furnace tubes, light diffusers, and lamp housings. Both types of quartz glass play a crucial role in various industries such as electronics, semiconductor, and telecommunications.</p></p>
