<p><p>ATH Flame Retardant Market Types include Ground/Milled ATH and Precipitated ATH. Ground/Milled ATH is produced by grinding natural aluminum hydroxide into finely sized particles, which are commonly used as fillers in various applications. On the other hand, Precipitated ATH is generated through a chemical precipitation process and is known for its higher purity and fine particle size. Both types of ATH Flame Retardants are utilized in industries such as plastics, rubber, textiles, and construction for their fire-retardant properties and effectiveness in reducing smoke emission during fires.</p></p>
