<p><p>Split fibers can be classified into two main types: Polyamide/ Polyester Combination and Polyester/ Polyolefines Combination. The Polyamide/ Polyester Combination split fibers provide a balance between durability and softness, making them ideal for use in applications like clothing, upholstery, and automotive interiors. On the other hand, Polyester/ Polyolefins Combination split fibers offer superior moisture-wicking properties and are commonly used in the production of technical textiles, sports apparel, and outdoor gear. Both types of split fibers cater to different market needs and applications.</p></p>
