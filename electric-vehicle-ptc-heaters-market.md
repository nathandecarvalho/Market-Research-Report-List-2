Immersion Heaters are submerged directly into the coolant of the vehicle to heat it quickly. Radiator Heaters are installed near the vehicle's radiator to warm the air passing through it. Pad Style Heaters are attached to the vehicle's battery or cabin floor to provide warmth. Each type of heater serves a different purpose in the electric vehicle heating system, offering efficient and effective heating solutions for electric vehicles.</p></p>
