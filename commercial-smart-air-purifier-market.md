<p><ul><li>HEPA Technology</li><li>Electrostatic Precipitators Technology</li><li>Ionizers And Ozone Generators Technology</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Commercial Smart Air Purifier market consists of different types of technologies such as HEPA, Electrostatic Precipitators, Ionizers, and Ozone Generators. HEPA technology uses a filter to trap particles like dust, pollen, and pet dander. Electrostatic Precipitators technology charges particles in the air and collects them on plates. Ionizers release negatively charged ions to attach to particles and remove them from the air. Ozone Generators technology releases ozone to purify the air but may have potential health risks.</p></p>
