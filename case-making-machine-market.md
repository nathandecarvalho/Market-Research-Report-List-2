<p><p>Case Making Machine Market can be categorized into three main types based on the different processes involved:</p><p>1. Material Cutting: Machines that are specifically designed for cutting materials such as paper, cardboard, or plastic to create the case components. These machines ensure precise and accurate cutting to meet the required specifications.</p><p>2. Mold Press Processing: Machines that use molds to press and shape the cut materials into the desired case form. This process helps in creating uniform, high-quality cases with consistent shapes and sizes.</p><p>3. Others: This category includes machines that offer additional features or perform different processes such as folding, gluing, or assembling the case components. These machines provide a complete solution for case making production.</p></p>
