<p><strong>The Underwater Night Vision Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Image Intensifier Night Vision</li><li>Thermal Image Night Vision</li><li>Digital Night Vision</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Underwater night vision technology includes three main types: image intensifier night vision, thermal image night vision, and digital night vision. Image intensifier night vision works by magnifying ambient light to enhance visibility in low-light conditions. Thermal image night vision detects heat signatures to produce images even in complete darkness. Digital night vision uses image sensors and processing algorithms to capture and display images in low-light environments. Each type has its own unique advantages and applications in underwater environments.</p></p>
