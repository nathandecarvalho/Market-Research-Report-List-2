<p><p>Carbon nanotubes (CNTs) for lithium battery market can be categorized into two main types: Single-walled Nanotubes (SWNTs) and Multi-walled Nanotubes (MWNTs). SWNTs consist of a single layer of carbon atoms arranged in a cylindrical structure, offering high strength and conductivity. Both types of CNTs are widely used in lithium battery applications due to their unique properties and performance advantages.</p></p>
