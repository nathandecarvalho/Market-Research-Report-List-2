<p><p>High-Reliability Ceramic Substrate market can be categorized into two main types: HTCC (High-Temperature Co-Fired Ceramic) Substrate and LTCC (Low-Temperature Co-Fired Ceramic) Substrate. HTCC substrates are designed for high-temperature applications where the material can withstand extreme heat conditions, making them suitable for use in aerospace and automotive industries. On the other hand, LTCC substrates are used in low-temperature applications due to their ability to provide excellent thermal conductivity, making them ideal for electronics and telecommunications industries.</p></p>
