<p><p>Abrasive metering valves are commonly used in a variety of applications such as sanding, sandblasting, shot blasting, and other abrasive material handling processes. In sander applications, the valve controls the flow of abrasive materials onto the surface being sanded. In sandblasting, the valve regulates the amount of abrasive material being propelled by the compressed air onto a surface. In shot blasting, the valve controls the flow of abrasive media used for cleaning, strengthening, or peening surfaces. The valve is also used in other applications where precise control of abrasive material flow is required.</p></p>
