<p><p>Silicon Carbide (SiC) fibers come in two main types: continuous and short fibers. Continuous SiC fibers are long, continuous strands that offer high strength, stiffness, and thermal conductivity, making them ideal for high-performance applications such as aerospace and automotive industries. On the other hand, short SiC fibers are shorter in length and are commonly used as reinforcement in composite materials to enhance their mechanical properties. Both types of SiC fibers play a crucial role in various advanced manufacturing processes.</p></p>
