<p><p>Immunohistochemistry testing market includes various methods like Traditional Direct Method, New Direct Method, Enzyme Bridge method, and PAP Method. Traditional Direct Method involves using a primary antibody followed by a labeled secondary antibody. The New Direct Method utilizes a labeled primary antibody for directly detecting antigens. Enzyme Bridge method involves using an enzyme-labeled secondary antibody for amplifying signals. PAP Method utilizes a peroxidase-labelled polymer for signal amplification. These methods are used in the detection of specific proteins in tissue samples for diagnostic and research purposes.</p></p>
