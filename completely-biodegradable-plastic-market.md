<p><p>Completely biodegradable plastic market is segmented into different types including thermoplastic starch plastic, aliphatic polyester, polylactic acid, starch/polyvinyl alcohol, and others. Thermoplastic starch plastic is made from renewable sources like corn or potatoes. Aliphatic polyester is derived from natural resources and breaks down quickly in the environment. Polylactic acid is made from fermented plant starches and is compostable. Starch/polyvinyl alcohol blends provide biodegradability and water resistance. The "others" category includes various biodegradable plastics made from different sources and processes.</p></p>
