<p><p>Animal-based compound feed market can be categorized into different types based on their physical form, such as mash, pellet, crumble, and other forms. Mash feed is in the form of a coarse powder, while pellet feed is compacted into small cylindrical shapes. Crumble feed is a combination of mash and pellets, with a finer texture. Other forms can include extruded feed or liquid feed. Each type of feed offers different benefits in terms of digestibility, feed efficiency, and ease of handling for livestock.</p></p>
