<p><p>Corrugated carton ink market offers three main types of inks: water-based inks, solvent-based inks, and UV-cured inks. Water-based inks are environmentally friendly and offer good adhesion to corrugated surfaces. Solvent-based inks provide strong color intensity and fast drying times, but may contain harmful chemicals. UV-cured inks are ideal for high-speed printing and offer superior resistance to scratching and fading. Each type of ink caters to different needs and preferences in the corrugated carton industry.</p></p>
