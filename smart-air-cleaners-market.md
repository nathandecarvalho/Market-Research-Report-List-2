<p><p>Smart air cleaners come in different types including HEPA filters, Electrostatic Precipitators, Ionizers, and Ozone Generators. HEPA filters use a dense filter to trap small particles, while Electrostatic Precipitators use an electric charge to remove particles from the air. Ionizers release negatively charged ions to attach to particles and remove them from the air. Ozone generators release ozone to neutralize odors and kill bacteria. Each type of air cleaner has its own unique technology for improving indoor air quality.</p></p>
