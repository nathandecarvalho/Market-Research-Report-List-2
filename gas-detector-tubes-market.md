<p><ul><li>Colorimetric Gas Detector Tubes</li><li>Dosimetric Gas Detector Tubes</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Gas detector tubes are used for quick and reliable measurement of specific gases in the air. Colorimetric gas detector tubes rely on a color change reaction to indicate the presence of a particular gas. Dosimetric gas detector tubes, on the other hand, measure cumulative exposure to a gas over time. Both types of tubes are widely used in various industries such as construction, mining, and manufacturing to ensure safety and compliance with regulations regarding gas exposure levels.</p></p>
