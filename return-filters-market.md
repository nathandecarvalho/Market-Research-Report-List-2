<p><p>Return filters are commonly used in various industries to remove contaminants from hydraulic systems. Two types of return filters in the market are tank-mounted return filters and semi-submerged return filters. Tank-mounted return filters are installed directly on top of the hydraulic tank, providing easy access for maintenance and replacement. On the other hand, semi-submerged return filters are partially submerged in the hydraulic tank, allowing for continuous filtration of the fluid. Both types play a crucial role in maintaining the cleanliness and efficiency of hydraulic systems.</p></p>
