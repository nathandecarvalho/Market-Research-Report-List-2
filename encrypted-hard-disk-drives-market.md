<p><p>Encrypted Hard Disk Drives come in two main market types: Hard Disk Drive FDE and Enclosed Hard Disk Drive FDE. Hard Disk Drive FDE refers to drives that have built-in encryption capabilities at the hardware level, ensuring that data is protected at all times. Both types cater to individuals and businesses seeking to safeguard their data from potential threats.</p></p>
