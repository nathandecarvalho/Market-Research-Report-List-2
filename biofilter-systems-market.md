<p><p>Biofilter systems are commonly categorized into three main types: Biological Aerated Biofilter Systems, Denitrification Biofilter Systems, and Activated Carbon Biofilter Systems. Biological Aerated Biofilter Systems use microorganisms to degrade organic pollutants, while Denitrification Biofilter Systems remove nitrogen compounds from wastewater. Activated Carbon Biofilter Systems utilize activated carbon to absorb and remove organic and inorganic pollutants from water. Each type of biofilter system offers unique advantages for treating different types of wastewater and contaminants, making them essential for maintaining water quality and environmental sustainability.</p></p>
