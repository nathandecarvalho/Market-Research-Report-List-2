<p><p>Jet air dryers are commonly available in two main types: floor-mounted and wall-mounted. Floor-mounted dryers are installed on the ground and often found in high-traffic public restrooms. They are easily accessible for all users and are usually more cost-effective. On the other hand, wall-mounted dryers are attached to the wall, saving floor space and providing a sleeker appearance. They are commonly used in smaller restrooms or where space is limited. Both types offer efficient hand drying with powerful jets of air.</p></p>
