<p><strong>The UV Cure Printing Inks Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Arc Curing</li><li>LED Curing</li></ul></p>
<p><p>UV cure printing inks are categorized into two main types based on the curing technology used: Arc Curing and LED Curing. Arc curing involves the use of traditional mercury arc lamps to produce high-intensity UV light for curing inks. On the other hand, LED curing utilizes light-emitting diodes to generate UV light for the curing process. LED curing is preferred for its energy efficiency, longer lifespan, and reduced heat output compared to arc curing. Both types offer fast curing times and improved print quality.</p></p>
