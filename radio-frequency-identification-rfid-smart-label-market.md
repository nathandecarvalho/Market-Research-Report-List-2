<p><p>RFID Smart Label market types are categorized based on the frequency at which they operate. Ultra-High Frequency RFID labels operate at frequencies between 860-960 MHz, providing long read ranges and high data transfer speeds. High Frequency RFID labels operate at 13.56 MHz, offering shorter read ranges and moderate data transfer speeds. Low Frequency RFID labels operate at 125-134 kHz, with the shortest read ranges but enhanced ability to work near metal or liquids. Each type caters to specific needs and applications in the market.</p></p>
