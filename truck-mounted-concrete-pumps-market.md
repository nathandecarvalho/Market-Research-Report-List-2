<p><p>Truck-mounted concrete pumps come in various types based on their reach capacity. Short arm pumps have a reach of 13-28 meters, making them suitable for small to medium construction projects. Long arm pumps have a reach of 31-47 meters, ideal for large construction sites. Long boom pumps, with a reach of 48 meters or more, are used for high-rise buildings and mega infrastructure projects. Each type caters to different project requirements, offering flexibility and efficiency in concrete pumping operations.</p></p>
