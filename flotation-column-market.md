<p><p>Flotation columns are a type of mineral processing equipment used for separating minerals from ore. The different types of flotation columns include countercurrent flotation columns, filling medium flotation columns, CPT flotation columns, and others. Countercurrent columns use a downward flow of water to separate the minerals, while filling medium columns use a dense medium to achieve separation. CPT columns employ centrifugal forces to enhance the flotation process. Other types of columns may use different methods or technologies to achieve mineral separation.</p></p>
