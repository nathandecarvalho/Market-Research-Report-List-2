<p><p>Heart valve retractors are surgical instruments used to hold, manipulate, and retract heart valves during cardiac surgeries. There are two main types of heart valve retractors: sliding type and rotating type. Sliding type retractors allow for linear movement to retract and hold the heart valve in place, while rotating type retractors have a rotating mechanism that enables 360-degree manipulation of the valve. Both types of retractors are vital tools in ensuring precise and efficient heart valve surgeries.</p></p>
