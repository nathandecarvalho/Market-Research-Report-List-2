<p><p>SAW filters are surface acoustic wave filters that use piezoelectric material to transmit and receive acoustic waves. They are commonly used in radio frequency devices due to their high quality factor and low insertion loss. BAW filters, on the other hand, or bulk acoustic wave filters, use bulk acoustic waves to filter out unwanted signals. They are known for their high frequency stability and low power consumption. Both SAW and BAW filters are widely used in telecommunications, consumer electronics, and automotive industries.</p></p>
