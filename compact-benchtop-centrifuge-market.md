<p><p>Compact benchtop centrifuges come in two main types, ventilated centrifuges and refrigerated centrifuges. Ventilated centrifuges are designed to dissipate heat generated during operation, making them suitable for applications where temperature control is not critical. In contrast, refrigerated centrifuges have a built-in cooling system that allows for precise temperature control, making them ideal for applications requiring samples to be kept at low temperatures. Both types offer efficient and reliable centrifugation for various laboratory tasks.</p></p>
