<p><p>Preclinical drug development services encompass various types such as API preparation, formulation studies, safety studies, and ADME studies. API preparation involves synthesizing the active pharmaceutical ingredient of a potential drug. Formulation studies focus on finding the most effective way to deliver the drug. Safety studies assess the potential risks associated with the drug. ADME studies evaluate the absorption, distribution, metabolism, and excretion of the drug in the body. These services are essential in assessing the efficacy and safety of a new drug before proceeding to clinical trials.</p></p>
