<p><p>Passenger vehicle airbag inflator market can be categorized into three types: Pyrotechnic Inflator, Stored Gas Inflator, and Hybrid Inflator. </p><p>1. Pyrotechnic Inflator: Utilizes a small explosive charge to rapidly release gas into the airbag during a collision.</p><p>2. Stored Gas Inflator: Uses compressed gas stored in a cylinder to inflate the airbag.</p><p>3. Hybrid Inflator: Combines elements of both pyrotechnic and stored gas systems to provide efficient and reliable inflation of the airbag in the event of a crash.</p></p>
