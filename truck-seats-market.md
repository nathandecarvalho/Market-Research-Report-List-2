<p><ul><li>Air Suspension Truck Seats</li><li>Mechanical Suspension Truck Seats</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Truck seats market consists of two main types: Air Suspension Truck Seats and Mechanical Suspension Truck Seats. Air suspension truck seats utilize compressed air to provide a smooth and comfortable ride by adjusting to the driver's weight and road conditions. On the other hand, mechanical suspension truck seats use springs and dampers to absorb shocks and vibrations, offering a more traditional approach to suspension. Both options aim to enhance driver comfort and reduce fatigue during long hours of driving.</p></p>
