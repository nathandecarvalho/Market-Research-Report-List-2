<p><strong>The Portable Speakers Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Active Speaker</li><li>Passive Speaker</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Portable speakers can be divided into two main market types: active speakers and passive speakers. Active speakers have a built-in power amplifier, making them self-powered and requiring only a power source to function. On the other hand, passive speakers do not have a built-in amplifier and need to be connected to an external amplifier or audio source to produce sound. Both types offer different features and benefits to cater to different consumer preferences in the portable speaker market.</p></p>
