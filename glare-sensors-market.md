<p><p>Glare sensors are used in automotive vehicles to detect and measure the intensity of glare from headlights, helping to adjust the brightness of the vehicle's interior lights accordingly. There are two main types of glare sensors on the market: Integrated sensors, which are built into the vehicle's existing systems, and Conventional sensors, which are standalone devices that can be easily installed. Integrated sensors are often preferred for their seamless integration with the vehicle's electronics, while Conventional sensors offer more flexibility in terms of placement and compatibility with different vehicles.</p></p>
