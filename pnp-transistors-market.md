<p><p>PNP transistors are a type of bipolar transistor that consist of three layers of a semiconductor material. They are commonly used in electronic devices for amplification and switching. Field-effect transistors, on the other hand, utilize an electric field to control the conductivity of the semiconductor material. They come in two main types: JFETs (Junction Field-effect Transistors) and MOSFETs (Metal-Oxide-Semiconductor Field-Effect Transistors). Both types of transistors play a crucial role in the electronics industry for various applications.</p></p>
