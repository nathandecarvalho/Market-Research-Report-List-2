<p><p>Arrayed Waveguide Grating (AWG) Devices are used in wavelength division multiplexing (WDM) systems to separate and combine different wavelengths of light. There are two main types of AWG devices in the market: Thermal AWG and Athermal AWG. Thermal AWGs require temperature control to maintain peak performance, while Athermal AWGs do not. Athermal AWGs are more cost-effective and easier to deploy, making them popular in a wide range of applications. Thermal AWGs are typically used in more demanding environments where temperature control is feasible.</p></p>
