<p><p>Synthetic Rubber Hot Melt Adhesives can be classified into two main types - High Temperature Vulcanizing (HTV) and Room Temperature Vulcanizing (RTV) adhesives. HTV adhesives require high temperatures for curing, making them suitable for applications where heat resistance is required. On the other hand, RTV adhesives cure at room temperature, offering convenience and flexibility in application. Both types of adhesives offer strong bonding properties and are commonly used in a variety of industries such as automotive, construction, and electronics.</p></p>
