<p><p>Acrylic yarn market consists of two main types: 100% Acrylic Yarn and Blended Acrylic Yarn. </p><p>100% Acrylic Yarn is made entirely of acrylic fibers, which provides excellent durability, softness, and ability to hold color well. It is widely used for various knitting and crocheting projects.</p><p>On the other hand, Blended Acrylic Yarn is a combination of acrylic fibers with other materials like wool or nylon. This type of yarn offers a balance of qualities from different fibers, such as warmth, softness, and elasticity.</p></p>
