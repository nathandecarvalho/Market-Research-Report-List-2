<p><p>The Commercial Car Driving Recorder market consists of two main types: portable and integrated recorders. Portable recorders, as the name suggests, are standalone devices that can be easily installed and removed from vehicles. They are ideal for businesses with small fleets or for personal use. Integrated recorders, on the other hand, are built-in systems that are permanently installed in vehicles. They are commonly used by larger commercial fleets for continuous monitoring and surveillance. Both types offer a range of features such as GPS tracking, real-time video recording, and driver behavior analysis.</p></p>
