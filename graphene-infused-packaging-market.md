<p><p>Graphene infused packaging comes in two main types: single layer and multiple layer. Single layer packaging consists of a single sheet of graphene that provides benefits such as increased strength, flexibility, and barrier properties. Multiple layer packaging, on the other hand, includes several layers of graphene that offer enhanced protection against external factors like moisture, oxygen, and UV radiation. Both types of graphene-infused packaging are being increasingly adopted in various industries for their superior performance and sustainability features.</p></p>
