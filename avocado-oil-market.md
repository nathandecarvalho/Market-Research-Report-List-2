<p><p>Avocado oil comes in three main types - Refined Avocado Oil, Extra Virgin Avocado Oil, and Crude Avocado Oil. Refined avocado oil is processed to remove impurities and has a neutral flavor, making it ideal for cooking at high temperatures. Extra virgin avocado oil is minimally processed, resulting in a rich flavor and high nutrient content. Crude avocado oil is unrefined and retains more of its natural properties, making it suitable for cosmetic and skincare applications. Each type offers unique benefits and uses in the market.</p></p>
