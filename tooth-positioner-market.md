<p><p>Tooth positioners are orthodontic devices used to adjust the alignment of teeth. Non-extraction positioners are designed to correct tooth alignment without the need for any teeth to be removed. Extraction positioners, on the other hand, are used when tooth extraction is necessary to create space for proper alignment. Both types of positioners help in moving teeth into the desired position and improving overall dental health. Patients may be prescribed either type based on their individual orthodontic needs and treatment plan.</p></p>
