<p><strong>The Automotive Stampings Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Cold Stamping</li><li>Hot Stamping</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Automotive stampings consist of two main types: cold stamping and hot stamping. Cold stamping involves the shaping and forming of metal sheets at room temperature, allowing for complex designs and tight tolerances. Hot stamping, on the other hand, involves heating the metal sheets before forming, resulting in a stronger and more durable final product. Cold stamping is commonly used for simpler parts, while hot stamping is preferred for components requiring high strength and crash resistance in the automotive industry.</p></p>
