<p><p>Elastomeric gasket market includes rubber and thermoplastic elastomers as its main types. Rubber gaskets are made from synthetic or natural rubber materials and are known for their flexibility and durability. Thermoplastic elastomers, on the other hand, are a blend of rubber and plastic materials, offering both the flexibility of rubber and the processability of plastic. Both types of gaskets are used in various industries for sealing applications due to their ability to withstand high temperatures, chemicals, and pressure.</p></p>
