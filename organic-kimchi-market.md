<p><p>Organic kimchi comes in various types catering to different preferences. Baechu-kimchi is the most popular type made with Napa cabbage and a spicy seasoning mix. Dongchimi is a refreshing radish water kimchi. Kkakdugi is a cubed radish kimchi with a crunchy texture. Pa-kimchi is made with scallions and has a milder flavor. Oi Sobagi is a cucumber kimchi filled with spicy seasonings. Each of these markets offers a unique twist on the traditional kimchi recipe, appealing to a wide range of tastes.</p></p>
