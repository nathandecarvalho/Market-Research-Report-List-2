<p><p>Vehicle radial tires can be categorized into three main types based on their construction: All Steel, Semi-steel, and Other. All Steel radial tires are made entirely of steel belts and ply, providing superior strength and durability for heavy-duty vehicles. Semi-steel radial tires have a combination of steel belts and fabric or polyester ply, offering a balance of performance and cost-effectiveness for passenger cars and light trucks. The Other category includes radial tires with alternative materials or construction methods, such as hybrid or eco-friendly options.</p></p>
