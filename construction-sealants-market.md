<p><p>Construction sealants are commonly categorized into two main types - Moisture Cured and Heat Cured. Moisture cured sealants are designed to cure by reacting with moisture in the air, making them ideal for outdoor applications. On the other hand, Heat Cured sealants require external heat sources to cure, offering faster curing times and better adhesion in certain applications. Both types offer unique advantages and are used in various construction projects depending on specific requirements and conditions.</p></p>
