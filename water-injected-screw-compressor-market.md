<p><p>Water Injected Screw Compressors are available in two main types in the market: Positive Displacement Compressors and Speed Compressors. Positive Displacement Compressors work by trapping and reducing the volume of air, while Speed Compressors operate by increasing the speed of air particles to create pressure. Positive Displacement Compressors are commonly used in applications where a constant flow of compressed air is required, while Speed Compressors are suitable for applications that require varying levels of air pressure. Both types offer efficient and reliable performance in industrial settings.</p></p>
