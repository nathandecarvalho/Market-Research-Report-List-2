There are three main types of Box Compression Testers - Bend Tester, Spring Tester, and Crush Tester. Bend Tester measures the resistance of the box to bending, Spring Tester evaluates the springback properties of the corrugated material, and Crush Tester assesses the overall compressive strength of the box. Each type serves a specific function in ensuring the durability and quality of corrugated boxes in the market.</p></p>
