<p><p>Thermal Infrared Sensors are classified into two main types based on their detection mechanism: Photon Detection Thermal Infrared Sensors and Thermal Detection Thermal Infrared Sensors. Photon Detection sensors use photon detectors to convert infrared radiation into electrical signals, while Thermal Detection sensors rely on detecting temperature differences to measure infrared radiation. Both types of sensors are widely used in various applications such as night vision, surveillance, industrial automation, and medical imaging, due to their ability to detect heat signatures and provide valuable insights in detecting objects or anomalies.</p></p>
