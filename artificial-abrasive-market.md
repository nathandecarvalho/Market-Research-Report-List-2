<p><strong>The Artificial Abrasive Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Corundum Abrasive</li><li>Silicon Carbide Abrasive</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Artificial abrasive market is divided into two main types - Corundum Abrasive and Silicon Carbide Abrasive. Corundum abrasive is typically made from aluminum oxide and is known for its hardness and durability, making it suitable for a wide range of applications including metal polishing and grinding. Silicon Carbide abrasive, on the other hand, is made from silicon carbide and is characterized by its high thermal conductivity and ability to cut through hard materials. It is commonly used in the manufacturing of high-performance ceramics and refractories.</p></p>
