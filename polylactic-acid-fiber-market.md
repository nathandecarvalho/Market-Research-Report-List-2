<p><strong>The Polylactic Acid Fiber Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Monomer Preparation</li><li>Polymerization Preparation</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Polylactic Acid (PLA) Fiber Market consists of two main types - Monomer Preparation and Polymerization Preparation. Monomer Preparation involves the production of lactic acid, which is the building block for PLA fiber. This process includes fermentation of corn starch or sugarcane to produce lactic acid. Polymerization Preparation involves the polymerization of lactic acid into PLA polymer, which is then spun into fibers for various applications. Both processes are crucial in the production of PLA fibers for the textile industry.</p></p>
