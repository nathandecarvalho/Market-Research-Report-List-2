<p><strong>The Fixed Thermal Scanners Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Short-Wave Infrared (SWIR)</li><li>Mid-Wave Infrared (MWIR)</li><li>Long-Wave Infrared (LWIR)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Fixed thermal scanners are classified into three types based on the wavelength they detect: Short-Wave Infrared (SWIR), Mid-Wave Infrared (MWIR), and Long-Wave Infrared (LWIR). SWIR scanners are commonly used for industrial and scientific applications due to their ability to penetrate fog, dust, and smoke. MWIR scanners are ideal for high-temperature monitoring in industries like metal processing and petrochemicals. LWIR scanners are widely used in security and surveillance systems for detecting humans and monitoring temperatures in buildings.</p></p>
