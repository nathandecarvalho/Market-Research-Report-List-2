<p><p>The Monobloc Engine Market comprises three main types: Cylinder Head, Cylinder Block, and Crankcase. </p><p>Cylinder Head is the top portion of the engine where the intake and exhaust valves are located. Cylinder Block is the main structure of the engine where cylinders are housed, and it also contains the crankshaft. Crankcase is the bottom portion of the engine that encloses the crankshaft and provides support to the moving parts.</p><p>Each component plays a crucial role in the overall functioning of the engine and contributes to its performance and durability.</p></p>
