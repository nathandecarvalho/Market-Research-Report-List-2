<p><p>Stainless steel alloy market comprises three main types: Austenitic, Ferritic, and Martensitic. Austenitic stainless steel is non-magnetic and contains high levels of nickel and chromium, making it highly resistant to corrosion. Ferritic stainless steel is magnetic, with lower levels of chromium, offering good corrosion resistance and high formability. Martensitic stainless steel is tough and hardenable through heat treatment, making it suitable for applications requiring high strength and wear resistance. Each type has its own unique properties and applications in different industries.</p></p>
