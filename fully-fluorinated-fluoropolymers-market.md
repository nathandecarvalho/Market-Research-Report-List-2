<p><p>Fully Fluorinated Fluoropolymers market consists of three main types: PTFE (Polytetrafluoroethylene), FEP (Fluorinated Ethylene Propylene), and PFA (Perfluoroalkoxy). PTFE is widely used for its non-stick properties and chemical resistance. FEP is known for its excellent thermal stability and transparency, making it suitable for wire and cable insulation. PFA combines the properties of both PTFE and FEP, offering improved durability and flexibility. These fluoropolymers are essential in industries such as automotive, electronics, and aerospace due to their unique combination of properties.</p></p>
