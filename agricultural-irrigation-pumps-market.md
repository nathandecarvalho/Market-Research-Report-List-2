<p><p>Agricultural irrigation pumps are essential for supplying water to crops in fields. The types of pumps commonly used in agricultural irrigation are submersible pumps, self-priming pumps, vortex pumps, and other specialty pumps. Submersible pumps are installed underwater and are suitable for deep wells. Self-priming pumps can operate without needing to be manually primed. Vortex pumps are designed to handle solid materials in the water. Other specialty pumps are used for specific irrigation needs, such as high-pressure or high-flow applications.</p></p>
