<p><p>Digital protective relays are devices used to protect electrical systems and equipment from potential damage or failure. They come in various types based on the specific application they are designed for. Overcurrent protection relays protect against excessive current flow, while arc protection relays safeguard against dangerous electrical arcs. Motor protection relays are used to protect motors from overheating or mechanical failures. Similarly, transformer protection relays, capacitor bank protection relays, and other specialized relays are designed to ensure the safe and efficient operation of different electrical components.</p></p>
