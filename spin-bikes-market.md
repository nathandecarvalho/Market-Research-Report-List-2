<p><strong>The Spin Bikes Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Magnetic Resistance</li><li>Felt Pad Resistance</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Spin bikes are available in two main resistance types: magnetic resistance and felt pad resistance. Magnetic resistance spin bikes use magnets to create resistance, resulting in a smoother and quieter ride. On the other hand, felt pad resistance spin bikes use friction between a felt pad and the flywheel to create resistance. While magnetic resistance bikes are generally more durable and require less maintenance, felt pad resistance bikes tend to be more affordable. Both types offer different riding experiences and cater to different preferences in the spin bike market.</p></p>
