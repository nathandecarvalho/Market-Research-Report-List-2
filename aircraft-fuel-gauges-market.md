<p><p>Aircraft fuel gauges can be categorized into two main types - analog type and digital type. Analog fuel gauges provide visual indications using a pointer or needle on a dial, while digital fuel gauges use numerical displays to show the amount of fuel remaining in the tank. Analog gauges are more traditional and easy to read at a glance, while digital gauges offer precise and accurate readings. Both types are commonly used in aircraft to monitor fuel levels during flight.</p></p>
