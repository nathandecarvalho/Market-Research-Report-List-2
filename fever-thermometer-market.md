<p><p>The fever thermometer market is divided into two types: non-contact and contact thermometers. Non-contact thermometers use infrared technology to measure body temperature from a distance without making direct contact with the skin. These thermometers are often preferred for their hygiene and convenience. On the other hand, contact thermometers require physical contact with the skin to measure temperature accurately. Both types of thermometers cater to different preferences and needs, with non-contact thermometers being more popular in certain settings for their ease of use.</p></p>
