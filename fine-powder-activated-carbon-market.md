<p><p>Fine Powder Activated Carbon Market consists of two main types, Physical Method and Chemical Method. The Physical Method involves using a high temperature to activate the carbon, which results in a porous structure. This type is known for its high adsorption capacity. On the other hand, the Chemical Method involves treating the carbon with chemicals to create a similar porous structure. Both types of activated carbon are widely used in industries such as water treatment, air purification, and food and beverage processing.</p></p>
