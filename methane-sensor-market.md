<p><ul><li>Infrared Sensor</li><li>Catalytic Bead Sensor</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Infrared sensor and catalytic bead sensor are two types of methane sensors used in the market. </p><p>Infrared sensor works by measuring the absorption of infrared light by methane molecules, providing accurate and reliable detection. </p><p>On the other hand, catalytic bead sensor uses a heated catalyst to oxidize methane, producing a change in resistance that indicates the presence of the gas. </p><p>Both sensors are commonly used for monitoring methane levels in various industrial applications such as oil and gas, mining, agriculture, and wastewater treatment.</p></p>
