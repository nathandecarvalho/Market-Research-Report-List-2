<p><p>Second-generation (2G) cellular M2M technology offers basic data transfer capabilities. Third-generation (3G) technology provides higher speeds and more advanced features like video calling and mobile internet access. Fourth-generation (4G)/Long-Term Evolution (LTE) technology offers even faster data transfer speeds and increased network capacity, making it ideal for applications requiring real-time data transmission such as autonomous vehicles and remote monitoring systems. Each generation represents advancements in cellular M2M technology, with 4G/LTE being the most advanced and efficient option available.</p></p>
