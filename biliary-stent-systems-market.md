<p><ul><li>Conventional Un-covered Stent</li><li>Hybrid weaving type Stent</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Biliary stent systems are used to treat obstructions in the bile duct. Conventional un-covered stents are made of metal or plastic and provide support to keep the duct open. Hybrid weaving type stents combine the benefits of covered and uncovered stents, with a woven design that offers both support and prevention of tissue ingrowth. These stents are commonly used in the treatment of bile duct strictures or blockages caused by conditions like cancer or pancreatitis.</p></p>
