<p><strong>The Ophthalmoplegia Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>External Ophthalmoplegia</li><li>Internuclear Ophthalmoplegia</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Ophthalmoplegia Market can be categorized into two types: External Ophthalmoplegia and Internuclear Ophthalmoplegia. External Ophthalmoplegia involves weakness or paralysis of the muscles that control eye movement, leading to difficulty in moving the eyes in different directions. Internuclear Ophthalmoplegia, on the other hand, is caused by a lesion in the brainstem that affects the connection between the cranial nerves responsible for eye movement. Both conditions can result in double vision, difficulty focusing, and issues with eye coordination. Treatment options may vary depending on the underlying cause of Ophthalmoplegia.</p></p>
