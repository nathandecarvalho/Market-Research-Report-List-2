<p><p>A Cycloidal Gearbox is a type of gearbox that uses cycloidal motion to transfer power from the input shaft to the output shaft. It consists of eccentric cams mounted on a disk, with rollers that move in a cycloidal motion to provide smooth and efficient power transfer.
