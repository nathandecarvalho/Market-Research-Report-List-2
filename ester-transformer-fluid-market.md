<p><p>Ester Transformer Fluid is classified into two main types: Natural Ester Transformer Fluid and Synthetic Ester Transformer Oil. Natural Ester Transformer Fluid is derived from natural sources such as vegetables or seeds, making it biodegradable and environmentally friendly. On the other hand, Synthetic Ester Transformer Oil is chemically synthesized and offers enhanced performance and stability compared to natural esters. Both types are used in power transformers to provide insulation and cooling, but synthetic esters are preferred in applications requiring higher performance levels.</p></p>
