<p><ul><li>Pretreatment Agent</li><li>Electroplating Additive</li><li>Post-treatment Agent</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Copper plating chemicals market consists of three main types - pretreatment agents, electroplating additives, and post-treatment agents. Pretreatment agents prepare the surface for plating by removing impurities and enhancing adhesion. Electroplating additives help in the deposition of copper onto the substrate, improving the quality and efficiency of the plating process. Post-treatment agents are used to protect the plated surface from corrosion and enhance its durability. Each type plays a crucial role in ensuring the success of copper plating applications.</p></p>
