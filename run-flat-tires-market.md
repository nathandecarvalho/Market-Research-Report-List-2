<p><p>Run flat tires are designed to allow a vehicle to continue driving even after a puncture or loss of tire pressure. There are two main types of run flat tires: self-supporting and support ring system. Self-supporting run flat tires have reinforced sidewalls that can bear the weight of the vehicle, while support ring system run flat tires use a supporting ring inside the tire to carry the load. Both types of run flat tires provide added safety and convenience by allowing drivers to continue driving to a safe location for repairs.</p></p>
