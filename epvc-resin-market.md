<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The EPVC Resin Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Homopolymeric EPVC</li><li>Co-polymeric EPVC</li></ul></p>
<p><p>EPVC resin market can be categorized into two main types: homopolymeric EPVC and co-polymeric EPVC. Homopolymeric EPVC is made from one type of vinyl chloride monomer and has uniform properties, making it suitable for rigid applications like pipes and fittings. On the other hand, co-polymeric EPVC is made from a mixture of vinyl chloride monomers and other comonomers, offering enhanced flexibility and impact resistance, making it ideal for applications such as automotive parts and medical devices. Both types cater to different market needs based on their specific properties and applications.</p></p>
