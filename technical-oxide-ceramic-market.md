<p><p>Technical oxide ceramic is a type of ceramic material that is produced by adding small amounts of metallic oxides to the base material to enhance its properties such as strength, hardness, and durability. These oxides can include alumina (Al2O3), zirconia (ZrO2), and silica (SiO2), among others. Technical oxide ceramics are widely used in industries such as electronics, aerospace, automotive, and healthcare due to their excellent thermal and chemical resistance.</p><p>The Technical Oxide Ceramic Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 4.6% during the forecast period.
