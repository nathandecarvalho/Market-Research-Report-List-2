<p><p>The Ceramic UF Membrane market consists of two main types: Tubular Membrane and Flat-sheet Membrane. Tubular Membrane technology involves a series of long, hollow tubes that allow for high filtration rates and easy cleaning. On the other hand, Flat-sheet Membrane technology uses a similar filtration process but in a flat configuration, providing a larger surface area for filtration. Both types are commonly used in water and wastewater treatment processes due to their efficiency and durability.</p></p>
