<p><p>Water-based floor paint encompasses two main categories: water-diluted paint and water dispersible paint. Water-diluted paint is a type of floor paint that is mixed with water before application to achieve the desired consistency. On the other hand, water dispersible paint is a type of floor paint that can be diluted with water even after it has dried, making it easier to clean up. Both types offer a more eco-friendly and low VOC alternative to traditional solvent-based floor paints.</p></p>
