<p><p>Multifunctional Dental Chairs come in two main types: Air Control Dental Chairs and Electronic Control Dental Chairs. Air Control chairs are operated using pneumatic controls which control functions such as patient positioning and chair movement. On the other hand, Electronic Control chairs utilize electronic systems to control the different chair functions, providing more precision and customization options. Both types of chairs offer a variety of features and settings to enhance the experience for both the dentist and the patient.</p></p>
