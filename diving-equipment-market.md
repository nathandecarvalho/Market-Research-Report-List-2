<p><p>The diving equipment market can be categorized into three main types: recreational diving, clearance diving, and saturation diving. Recreational diving equipment is designed for leisure diving activities such as snorkeling and scuba diving. Clearance diving equipment is used for underwater construction, maintenance, and salvage operations. Saturation diving equipment is specially designed for deep-sea diving operations where divers are exposed to high pressure for extended periods of time. Each market segment has specific equipment requirements tailored to the needs of divers in different environments.</p></p>
