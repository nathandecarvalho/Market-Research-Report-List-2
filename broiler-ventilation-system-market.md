<p><strong>The Broiler Ventilation System Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Ridge Ventilation System</li><li>Length Ventilation System</li><li>Tunnel Ventilation System</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Broiler ventilation systems are classified into different types based on their design and functionality. Ridge ventilation systems are installed along the ridge of the poultry house to allow hot air to escape. Length ventilation systems run the length of the house and are effective in removing heat and moisture. Tunnel ventilation systems create air movement through the house to provide a cooling effect. Other types of ventilation systems may include combination systems or customized designs based on specific requirements of the poultry house.</p></p>
