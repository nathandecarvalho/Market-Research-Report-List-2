<p><p>VoIP market types refer to different ways in which Voice over Internet Protocol services can be used. Computer-to-Computer VoIP allows users to make calls from one computer to another over the internet. Computer-to-Phone VoIP enables calls from a computer to a traditional phone number. Phone-to-Phone VoIP involves making calls between two traditional phone numbers using an internet connection. Each market type caters to different user preferences and needs for making voice calls using internet technology.</p></p>
