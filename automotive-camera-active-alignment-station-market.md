<p><p>The Automotive Camera Active Alignment Station market includes different types based on the degrees of freedom (DOF) they offer for alignment. 4 DOF stations allow movement in four directions, typically up/down and left/right. 5 DOF stations add the ability to rotate the camera, in addition to the basic movements. 6 DOF stations provide the most flexibility by including an additional degree of freedom, usually pitch or yaw. Each type caters to different alignment needs and preferences in the automotive industry.</p></p>
