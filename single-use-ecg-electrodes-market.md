<p><p>Single Use ECG Electrodes are available in two main types: Flat Paper-thin Sticker and Self-adhesive Circular Pad. Flat Paper-thin Stickers are designed to be easily applied to the skin and have a low profile to reduce interference during ECG monitoring. Self-adhesive Circular Pads are convenient for quick application and provide a secure attachment to the skin. Both types of electrodes are disposable, eliminating the need for cleaning and sterilization between uses, making them a hygienic and cost-effective option for healthcare facilities.</p></p>
