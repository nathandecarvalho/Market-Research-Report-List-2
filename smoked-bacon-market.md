<p><p>Smoked bacon comes in two main market types: hot smoked bacon and cold smoked bacon. Hot smoked bacon is cooked at higher temperatures (around 165-200°F) for a shorter period of time, giving it a more cooked texture and flavor. Cold smoked bacon, on the other hand, is smoked at lower temperatures (around 80-100°F) for a longer period of time, resulting in a more subtle and delicate smoky flavor. Both types of smoked bacon are popular choices for adding a rich and savory flavor to various dishes.</p></p>
