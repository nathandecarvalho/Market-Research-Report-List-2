<p><strong>The Sapphire Lenses Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Concave Lens</li><li>Convex Lens</li><li>Planoconvex Lens</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Sapphire lenses come in various types to suit different optical applications. Concave lenses curve inward, causing light rays to diverge. Convex lenses bulge outward, converging light rays to a focal point. Planoconvex lenses have one flat and one outward-curved surface, focusing light like convex lenses. Each type has unique properties and uses in industries such as aerospace, medical, and defense. Whether for magnification, focusing, or correction, sapphire lenses offer durability, high transparency, and excellent optical performance.</p></p>
