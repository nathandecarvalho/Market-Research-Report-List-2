<p><p>Draught fans are categorized into two main market types based on their pressure levels: low-pressure fans and high-pressure fans. Low-pressure fans are typically used for applications such as ventilation, air conditioning, and combustion air supply as they are designed to move large volumes of air at a relatively low pressure. On the other hand, high-pressure fans are used in industrial processes that require the movement of air or gases at higher pressure levels, such as pneumatic conveying, air filtration, and combustion systems.</p></p>
