<p><p>Skim Milk Powder (SMP) is categorized into different types based on the heat treatment it undergoes during processing. Low Heat SMP is produced at a lower temperature, retaining more of the milk's natural flavor and color. Medium Heat SMP is processed at a slightly higher temperature, offering a balance between flavor retention and bacterial inhibition. High Heat SMP is produced at a high temperature, resulting in a longer shelf life and better solubility, but with a slightly cooked flavor. These different types cater to diverse consumer preferences and market demands.</p></p>
